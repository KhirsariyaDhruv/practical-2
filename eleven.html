About This Portal

Welcome to the Charusat Student Portal – your one-stop platform for accessing academic records, submitting coursework, checking attendance, and staying updated with important notices and events.

Our goal is to simplify your academic journey by providing an intuitive and reliable portal where students and faculty can interact smoothly. Whether it’s registering for a course, viewing your grades, or contacting the admin – it’s all just a click away.

Thank you for using the Charusat Student Portal. We wish you success and happiness in your academic journey!
